About Us

"Welcome to "Yuvraj General Store", your neighborhood kirana store where convenience and affordability meet.

We have been serving the local community for over 35+ years and have built a reputation for providing high-quality products at unbeatable prices. Our mission is to make grocery shopping easy and accessible for everyone, by providing a wide range of products under one roof and ensuring that our customers always find what they need.

At "Yuvraj General Store", we pride ourselves on offering a wide selection of high-quality products at affordable prices. From fresh produce and groceries to household items and personal care products, we have everything you need to keep your household running smoothly.

We take great care in sourcing our products from the best suppliers, to ensure that our customers have access to the freshest and highest-quality groceries. Our store is regularly restocked with fresh dairy products, packaged goods, and more. We also offer a wide variety of household essentials and personal care products, making it easy for our customers to do all their shopping in one convenient location.

We are proud to be a part of the local community and are committed to providing our customers with the best possible shopping experience. Thank you for choosing "Yuvraj General Store". We look forward to serving you and your family for all your kirana needs."
